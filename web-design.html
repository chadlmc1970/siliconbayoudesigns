Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a custom website cost? +

Our custom websites start at $2,500 for a 5-page responsive site. Professional packages with full SEO strategy start at $5,000. Every project is scoped individually after a free consultation.

How long does it take to build a custom website? +

A typical 5-page website takes 3-4 weeks from kickoff to launch. More complex projects with e-commerce or custom features may take 6-8 weeks. You see progress every step of the way.

Do I own the code and design? +

Yes. You own 100% of the code, design, and content we create for you. No licensing fees, no lock-in, no restrictions.

What's the difference between your sites and WordPress? +

Our sites are hand-coded in clean HTML, CSS, and JavaScript - no WordPress, no page builders, no plugin bloat. This means faster load times (under 2 seconds vs 3-8 for WordPress), better SEO, stronger security, and zero dependency on third-party plugins.

Can I update the site myself after launch? +

Yes. We provide training and documentation so you can make content updates. For clients who prefer hands-off management, we offer monthly Website Care Plans starting at $150/month.
