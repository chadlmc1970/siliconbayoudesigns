Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does iOS app development cost? +

Our iOS app development starts at $10,000. Pricing scales with scope - a simple utility app costs less than a full-featured platform. We scope your project precisely after a free discovery call.

How long does it take to build an iOS app? +

A typical iOS app takes 8-16 weeks from kickoff to App Store submission, depending on complexity. We use sprint-based development with weekly demos so you see progress throughout.

Do I need an Apple Developer Account? +

Yes, you'll need an Apple Developer Account ($99/year) to publish on the App Store. We'll guide you through the setup and handle the submission process for you.

Can you update my existing iOS app? +

Yes. We can take over maintenance and updates for existing iOS apps, or rebuild them using modern Swift and SwiftUI. We'll assess your current codebase and recommend the best path forward.

What about Android? +

We specialize in native iOS development using Swift and SwiftUI. For projects that need both iOS and Android, we can recommend trusted Android partners or discuss cross-platform options during discovery.
