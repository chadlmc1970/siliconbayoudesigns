Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 1, 2026

Silicon Bayou Designs LLC ("we", "us", "our") operates the website siliconbayoudesigns.com. This page explains what data we collect when you visit our site and how we use it.

Analytics We Use

We use two analytics services to understand how visitors interact with our site so we can improve it:

  • Google Analytics 4 (GA4) — Collects anonymous usage data such as pages visited, time on site, device type, and approximate geographic region. We have IP anonymization enabled. Google Analytics uses cookies to distinguish unique visitors.
  • Microsoft Clarity — Records anonymized session replays and generates heatmaps showing where visitors click and scroll. Clarity does not collect personally identifiable information. It uses cookies to track sessions.

What Data We Collect

  • Pages you visit and how long you spend on them
  • Device type, browser, and screen size
  • Approximate location (city/region level, never precise)
  • Clicks and scroll behavior (anonymized)
  • Referral source (how you found our site)

We do not collect your name, email, or any personal information through analytics. If you submit our contact form, the information you provide (name, email, project details) is sent to us via Formspree and used only to respond to your inquiry.

Cookies

Our analytics tools set small cookies in your browser to distinguish visitors and track sessions. These are first-party cookies tied to our domain. We do not use advertising cookies or sell data to third parties.

How to Opt Out

  • Use your browser's "Do Not Track" setting
  • Install the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on
  • Use a browser extension that blocks tracking scripts
  • Clear cookies in your browser settings at any time

Third-Party Services

Our site uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Analytics — Google Privacy Policy
  • Microsoft Clarity — Microsoft Privacy Statement
  • Formspree (contact form) — Formspree Privacy Policy
  • Google Fonts — loaded from Google servers for typography

Data Retention

Google Analytics data is retained for 14 months. Microsoft Clarity retains session data for 30 days.

Children's Privacy

Our site is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, email us at hello@siliconbayoudesigns.com.
