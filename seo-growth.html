Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does SEO take to work? +

SEO is a long-term strategy. You'll typically see initial indexing improvements in 1-3 months, early ranking movement for commercial terms in 3-6 months, and meaningful organic traffic growth in 6-12 months with consistent effort.

Do you guarantee rankings? +

No legitimate SEO professional can guarantee specific rankings - Google's algorithm considers hundreds of factors. What we guarantee is a systematic, data-driven approach with transparent reporting so you can see exactly what's working.

What's included in SEO services? +

Our SEO engagement includes a full technical audit, keyword research and mapping, on-page optimization, schema markup, content strategy, Google Analytics and Search Console setup, competitor analysis, and monthly ranking reports.

Can I do SEO myself? +

Basic SEO fundamentals can be self-taught, but competitive rankings require technical expertise, consistent content creation, and ongoing optimization. We provide the strategy and execution so you can focus on running your business.

What's the difference between SEO and paid ads? +

Paid ads (PPC) deliver immediate traffic but stop when you stop paying. SEO builds organic traffic that compounds over time - once you rank, that traffic is essentially free. SEO typically delivers 5x the ROI of paid ads over a 12-month period.
